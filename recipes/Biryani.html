Biryani

Biryani is one of the best dish ever. It has a taste that is out of this world. I cannot praise it enough.

Period!

Ingredients

For Chicken Marination

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • Salt (According to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Juice of 1 lemon

For Rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 whole cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon
  • Salt (According to taste)

For Assembling

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Fried onions
  • mint and coriander leaves for garnish

Cooking

  1. Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs; let sit for at least 1 hour.
  2. Rinse, soak, and partially cook basmati rice with spices.
  3. Cook marinated chicken until tender and oil separates.
  4. Spread partially cooked rice over chicken; top with fried onions and herbs.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Fluff the biryani and serve hot with raita.
