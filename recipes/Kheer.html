Kheer

Kheer is dessert that I can never say no to. The khoyaness in it just makes it otherwordly.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds and cashews)
  • 1 tablespoon ghee

Cooking

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Boil milk, add rice, and simmer until thickened (20-25 minutes).
  3. Stir in sugar and condensed milk; cook for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Add cardamom and nuts; mix well.
  5. Cool slightly and serve warm or chilled, garnished with additional nuts.
